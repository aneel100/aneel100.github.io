Enver Creek is a secondary school located on 144 street and 84 avenue in Surrey, British Columbia. This school is for grades 8-12.
Enver Creek was founded in 1997. The current principal of Enver Creek is Mr. Kilpatrick.
The mascot for Enver Creek is the cougar. Classes start at 8:30 am and end at 2:52 pm. The main language at this school is English.
